# Mushroom
- A mushroom believed to be grown from the tears of the [[Oracle of Change]]
	- Also believed that it can give you visions of the future
## Properties
- Shifting blue-green colors
- Max 1 inch tall
- Hallucinogenic
- If your connection to the Fae is strong enough, you may see visions of the possible futures
## Where to find
- Temperate rainforest climates
- Abandoned bird nests in tall trees
## When to find
- During the summer months after a heavy rain
