# MBSC
_Wondrous Item, Artifact (requires attunement by [[High Mage Radelia Qinqen]], [[Aldarian Woods]], or [[Xerxes (fake) - Fleet Fibblewibble]])_

The MBSC is a work-in-progress and when tested can be dangerous. In the first stage of the MBSC, those not used to magical effects suffer particularly harsh effects. In the first stage, the MBSC is only able to cast the [Slow](https://2014.5e.tools/spells/slow-phb.html) spell. As [[High Mage Radelia Qinqen]] and [[The Handsome Fae (AKA Moonbean)|THF]] work on it, the range of spells it is able to cast grows. It is only able to cast utility spells, and not any damage spells, like fireball.

This massive arcane machine glows with an eerie bioluminescent blue light, bathing the surrounding area in a wavering, water-like glow. The device resembles a **15-foot-tall peony bud**, its form divided into **nine equal segments** by thick metal bands.

The MBSC is currently in **Stage 1**, functioning only as a minimally viable prototype.

The MBSC requires a **living conduit**. A White Mask or the attuned user must stand within the device’s activation field and maintain line of sight to the target location for the duration of the charge.

### Stage 1 — Stasis Pulse

The machine can cast the **Slow** spell over a vast area.

To activate this ability, the MBSC must charge for **10 minutes**, drawing in and draining the ambient magical energy of the surrounding region.

Once charged, the MBSC casts **Slow**, targeting a location within **20 miles**. The spell affects a circular area with a radius of up to **100–150 feet**, roughly covering **1–2 acres**..

After the spell is released, the magical aura of the surrounding region is temporarily depleted. An effect similar to **Antimagic Field** fills a **180-foot radius around the machine** for **1 minute**.

The MBSC can be used **once per day** while in Stage 1. Additional attempts to activate the device automatically fail.

If **Aldarian Woods** or **Xerxes** come into contact with the machine, they instantly attune to it, ending any previous attunement.
