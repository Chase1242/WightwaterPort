# Overview
- Wood is an essential export of Wightwater, primarily produced by a guild of druids who grow the trees in accordance to harmony in nature. The wood is high quality and highly sought after across the world and continent.
- The wood trade of Wightwater has abruptly stopped, with a short, non-descriptive announcement made by the Guild.
	- "We are undergoing internal reviews of processes to further increase output of wood while respecting the nature and cycles behind it"
## [[Talisolvanar 'Tally' Fellbranch]]
- Tally will inform the party that unfortunately, he will be unable to deliver on the promised goods for the foreseeable future, as all wood brought into the city has been stopped.
## The disease of the forest
- The forest, while not noticeable at first, seems to be somewhat diseased
- Some of the foliage dying back, some of the trees dropping their leaves early, unnaturally
- This is caused by [[Cailleach Mháthair]] and her presence (along with [[Kronos]]')
- The [[Crann Grove]] believes the [[Woodkin Druid's Grove]] is to blame for this
