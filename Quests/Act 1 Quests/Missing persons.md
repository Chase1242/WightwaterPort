# Overview
- There have been mysterious disappearances on [[Greathorn Street]] in the [[Norslums]]
- There are a couple disgruntled neighbors:
	- A dwarf gem cutter, Ginn Stonearm
	- A retired sailor Tortle, Yom Hardshell
	- A Merfolk diver that sells treasures found in [[Oria's Harbor]] 
## What actually happened
- People in the Norslums have been on edge since a beloved member of the community, who fights for justice for the working class, has gone missing. Some grumble conspiracies, like the Ebrosian nobility had them disposed of. 
	- This is not the case, however. Unfortunately, Kayla Lightheart, a sweet halfling, was abducted by a DIFFERENT cult in the city, one that follows a god like Bhaal (or some other undecided god), and is used as a ritual sacrifice for their god
- Other people have been disappearing, and the City Watch, already burdened with the gang war between the Zhents, have shelved this particular problem, especially since it happens outside the city walls and the problem is affecting the poorer people more than nobles
	- This causes unrest outside the city, with tensions between the guard and city folk raising higher and higher
## The [[Adventurer's Guild]]
- The Adventurer's Guild has put out two contracts
	- One for figuring out why and how (or where) these people are going missing - this is available to Tier 1 adventurer's)
	- One for solving the root cause of the issue (this is available to high Tier 2 and up adventurers)
## Ginn Stonearm
- Ginn is a weathered dwarf, who moved to Wightwater years ago, after enduring a brutal job in a mine for years. It's always been her personal dream to start a gem cutting/ delicate metalworking business, but life circumstances forced her into a dead-end job
- Ginn's neighbor, Dawn Fleetfoot, is the one kidnapped
	- Dawn's apartment has been sectioned off with "Do not enter" signs and official paperwork stating that the guard will investigate at it's earliest possible time
	- The other night, Ginn heard was awoken with a shrill scream that cut off sharply, followed quickly by footsteps down stairs. Ginn only saw four silhouetted figures carrying a limp body the moon highlighted to be an unconscious Dawn. The other four were masked, but of an average height. Ginn regrets not pursuing them to help Dawn, but she her knees were shaking so bad her legs wouldn't move and her heart was pounding loud enough to drown out other sounds from the night
- She quietly walked to a window to see the four disappear down an alley she knows as a dead end
## Each victim's living quarters
- The living quarters of the victims were always apartments or houses
- They were all alone and/or single
- They were kidnapped in the night
- The was some commotion to alert nearby folks, that was silenced abruptly
- Only Ginn saw them walk down a dead-end alley
- Investigation of the living quarters reveals a note that reads "Under vigilance and veil we come. Only after will you know true glory"
## The Sewers
- Some walls are marked with stylized eclipses - a moon sliding in front of the sun (or rather, a crescent sun)
- Following those paths reveals stairs into deep, pitch blackness.
- On a successful `22 DC perception check` someone notices grooves in a wall that sort of outline some form of door. There is no visible key or handle or anything.
	- Behind the secret doors are the kidnapped peoples (including the next adventurer's that are crying out for the current ones to save them)
