# How to fight underwater
## How are you:
### Breathing
- Aquatic race/ able to breath underwater fine
- Holding your breath
	- You can hold your breath a number of minutes equal to your Constitution mod, min 1, if not doing anything
	- Anything strenuous is Con mod equal to the number of rounds, min 1
- If you are still underwater when your time runs out, you drop to 0 hit points, you can't regain hit points, you can't be stabilized, and you can't succeed on death saving throws
- Air bubble, cap of water breathing
	- If gritty, con saves against poison if they come up too fast (bends)
- Water breathing
	- Allows you to breath water, but speaking is more difficult
		- Verbal component spells, roll a d100, 10% chance to cast a different spell
			- Same action economy, school of magic, casting time
		- 10% chance of a very similar spell being cast
### Seeing
### Moving
- If swim speed, that is fine
- Half movement otherwise
- Can handwave, once per combat a PC can just move their full speed
### Fighting
- Thrusting weapon, no disadvantage
- Swinging, disadvantage
- No slings
- Stringed, ranged weapons have disadvantage
- Thrown weapons at range, no disadvantage
- All fire damage is halved (not resisted)
- Acid/ poison
	- double radius (single person and one person adjacent)
	- half damage
- Cold damage
	- Target speed is reduced by 10 feet
- Lightning (in salt water)
	- Pick another target at random (including the caster), and they take half damage of the spell
- Radiant
	- Pick someone withing range of that person and they become blinded for the rest of the current turn
- Thunder
	- Additional d12 damage
- Psychic/ force/ necromantic damage
	- none
