# Caesar Cipher
- A simple cipher that maps from the regular alphabet to an alphabet shifted by some constant number. 
- [[High Mage Radelia Qinqen]] employs a much more complicated cipher in the game - but my players are silly so they wouldn't be able to decipher it. The current alphabet is +13

The mapping (+13):
a b c d e f g h i j k l m n o p q r s t u v w x y z
m n o p q r s t u v w x y z a b c d e f g h i j k l
