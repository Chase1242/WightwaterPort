# Knight Bears
- Knight bears are loosely based on sea bears from SpongeBob
## Rules
- Don't play a stringed instrument in the forest, at night
- Don't wave your flashlight back and forth really fast
- Don't stomp around, they take that as a challenge
- Don't eat cubed cheese
- Don't wear green on Aquesday in a serious fashion
- No clown shoes or hoop skirts
- Never screech like a chimpanzee
## Description
- Knight Bears are said to be valiant protectors of the Wolves and Beasts, subordinate to the [[King of Wolves and Beasts]]
- They are different types of bears, from Owl Bears to regular Bears, sometimes even Werebears
- Usually awakened by the power of the King of Wolves and Beasts
- Not limited to bears
- You notice they seem to have a small vestigial fin tail that doesn't really make any sense...
